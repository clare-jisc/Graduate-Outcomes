Currently surveying graduates who completed an HE course between November 2022 - January 2023


The Graduate Outcomes survey exists to improve the experience of future students.

That’s why we want to hear from you. We’d like to understand more about your career path and experiences after leaving higher education.

We ask all graduates to share their perspectives, 15 months after they finish their course, to build a national picture that UK universities and colleges use to innovate their services. League tables, Prospects and The Complete University Guide use our Graduate Outcomes data to help students make decisions about their future.
